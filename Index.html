How long visa processing dey take?+

Dubai visas typically land within 24–72 hours once your documents complete. Umrah, Malaysia and Morocco vary by season — message us with your travel date and we go give you the honest timeline, no packaging.

Wetin dey inside the packages?+

Flights, hotel, visa, airport pickup and the headline activities (desert safari, city tour, beach resort — depending on destination). Tell us your budget and headcount, we go build the package around am.

Can I pay in naira?+

Yes o. Naira transfer, dirhams, or card — we go send you the account details and a clear breakdown before you pay anything.

Una dey do group trips?+

Our specialty! Detty December crews, honeymooners, office retreats, family Umrah — groups actually get better hotel and activity rates, so gather your people.

We no dey issue passports, but we go walk you through the application step by step and plan your trip so e ready the moment your passport lands.
